Isaac Newton - A Famous Scientist

Isaac Newton

Autobiography

Sir Isaac Newton was born on 25 December 1642 and died on 20 March 1726/27. He was an English polymath who profoundly impacted various fields of science. Newton's curiosity and perseverance led to groundbreaking discoveries that still influence modern science. His work in mathematics and physics laid the foundation for classical mechanics and calculus. Reflecting on his life, one can see the profound impact of his contributions on the world as we know it today.
